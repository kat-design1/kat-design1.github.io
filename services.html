What we do
Aside from sharing adventures and education on Instagram (a real life with eco mistakes and all!). We love to share values and learnings with anyone right from childhood through to elderly. We provide a range of value to suit different levels, interests and stage in your ethical living journey. We are passionate about hosting events and worrkshops on all things ethical and sustainable. But sharing this lifestyle often means finding products and services that care! I work with sustainably minded brands to shout their name from the roof tops on my blog and social media. As well as consulting with companies to help them be better.

Speaking/Events

Product launch? Professional development session? High school session? Liven up your weekly team meeting? I have spoken in all kinds of situations, and can tailor my session to suit you. From TEDx speaking, to fabric tech chats, I can speak wherever and however you want me to!
